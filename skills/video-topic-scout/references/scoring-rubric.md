# Topic Scoring Rubric

Score each category from 1-5. Recommend topics above 20/30.

| Category | Question |
| --- | --- |
| Audience pain | Does the viewer already feel this problem? |
| Novelty | Is there a fresh angle, tool, example, or timing? |
| Proof | Can the claim be supported with visible evidence? |
| Format fit | Can it be explained visually in the target runtime? |
| Monetization fit | Does it lead to an offer, service, product, or portfolio proof? |
| Production feasibility | Can the video be made with available assets and time? |

## Red Flags

- "AI will change everything" with no concrete viewer.
- Tool news with no use case or business consequence.
- Pure opinion with no proof path.
- Topics that depend on private client data.
- Claims about income, health, finance, or law without careful caveats and sources.

## Useful Topic Patterns

- "I tried X so you do not have to."
- "The hidden cost of Y."
- "Before/after workflow transformation."
- "New tool, old business problem."
- "One source, many outputs."
- "What most people get wrong about X."
