# Channel Matrix

## YouTube Shorts

- Best for: broad discovery, tutorials, workflow transformations.
- Runtime: under 60s preferred.
- Needs: strong first frame, keyword title, subtitles.
- CTA: subscribe, full tutorial, lead magnet.

## TikTok

- Best for: pattern interrupts, creator personality, fast experiments.
- Needs: native-feeling hook, informal language, quick pacing.
- CTA: comment prompt or follow for series.

## Instagram Reels

- Best for: polished visual snippets, portfolio aesthetics, creator brand.
- Needs: clean captions, strong cover frame.
- CTA: DM, save, profile link.

## X Video

- Best for: tech builders, AI workflows, founder/operator audiences.
- Needs: sharp post copy, visible proof, thread follow-up.
- CTA: repo link, demo link, reply prompt.

## LinkedIn Video

- Best for: B2B services, consultants, training, enterprise workflows.
- Needs: business framing, concrete outcome, professional thumbnail.
- CTA: consultation, case study, newsletter.

## YouTube Long-Form

- Best for: tutorials, case studies, deep dives.
- Runtime: 3-12m for most practical demos.
- Needs: searchable title, chapters, source links.
- CTA: repo, template, newsletter.

## Landing Page / Portfolio

- Best for: service proof and sales enablement.
- Needs: clear offer, no distracting platform memes, evergreen language.
- CTA: inquiry, booking, demo request.
